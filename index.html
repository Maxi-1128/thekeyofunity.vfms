The Key of Unity

An Original Harry Potter Stage Play

Step into a world where friendship, courage, and the bonds between houses will be tested. Deep within the halls of Hogwarts, an ancient magical artifact has been rediscovered — and it may hold the power to unite … or to divide Hogwarts forever.

About the Play

Written and Directed by Songhao (Maxi) Zhang, "The Key of Unity" is a thrilling journey into the heart of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Drawing inspiration from the Wizarding World created by J.K. Rowling, this stage production introduces original challenges, unexpected alliances, and the magic of house unity.
