Tickets

Tickets are required to attend the performance and can be purchased online here, or in person at the VFMS on the performance date(s). Ticket prices are FREE for all students and staff of VFMS, and $5 for all other audience. Please note: all proceeds from donations and tickets will go directly toward covering the costs of the play (such as costumes, sets, and venue expenses) and WILL NOT be used for profit.

Tickets:

You are REQUIRED to sign yourself up prior to the performance. You may sign up for multiple people, BUT you must submit each person SEPARATELY. Please do not submit yourself twice. Contact us if you are unsure if you've already signed up. Thank you!
