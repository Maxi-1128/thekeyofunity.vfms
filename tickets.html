Tickets

Tickets are required to attend the performance and can be purchased online here, or in person at the VFMS Library at ALL TIMES. You can also receive tickets at the PERFORMANCE. Ticket prices are FREE for all. You may donate to the performance ON THE DATE OF THE PERFORMANCE. Please note: all proceeds from donates and sales will go directly toward covering the costs of the play (such as costumes, sets, and venue expenses) and WILL NOT be used for profit.

Tickets:

You are REQUIRED to sign yourself up prior to the performance. You may sign up for multiple people, BUT you must submit each person SEPARATELY. Please try and not submit yourself twice. Thank you!
